About Lauren

I'm Lauren Leibowitz, Entertainment Editor at Thrillist. In my spare time, I like reading books (fiction and non-), riding bikes (road and stationary), and streaming television (all kinds). In past lives, I've been a copy editor for NYMag.com, where I specialized in Vulture content; done blogging stints for Vulture and the Huffington Post's Style vertical; contributed restaurant and bar listings for New York Magazine's website; written features for Mic.com, Bedford & Bowery, TeenVogue.com, and the Creators Project; and copyedited for a diverse roster of clients, including Eater.com, Lucky Peach, and various book authors.
