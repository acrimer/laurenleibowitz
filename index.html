About Lauren

I'm Lauren Leibowitz, a writer and editor for hire based in New York City. By day, I copyedit for NYMag.com, where I specialize in Vulture content; by night, you can catch me reading fiction, biking around town, or watching highbrow television.

I graduated from NYU's Gallatin School in 2012 with an interdisciplinary degree in writing and art, a minor in creative writing, and Honors Scholar distinction; before transferring to Gallatin in 2011, I attended Boston University's College of Communication, where I studied magazine journalism and completed a linguistics minor.

I've done blogging stints for Vulture and the Huffington Post's Style vertical; contributed restaurant and bar listings for New York Magazine's website; written features for Mic.com, Bedford & Bowery, TeenVogue.com, and the Creators Project; and copyedited for a diverse roster of clients, including Eater.com, Lucky Peach, and various book authors.
